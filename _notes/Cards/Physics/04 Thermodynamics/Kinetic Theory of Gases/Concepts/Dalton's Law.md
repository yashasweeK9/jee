### Form of Equations of Dalton's Law of Partial Pressure
>![](https://i.imgur.com/Ef67FqK.png)

1. >$$P_T = P_1 + P_2$$
2. >$$P_1 = P_1X_1$$
3. >$$P_2 = P_TX_2$$
4. >$$\frac{P_1}{P_2} = \frac{n_1}{n_2}$$
