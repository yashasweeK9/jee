## General Equation #important #commit-to-memory
1. Equation of the Line
>$$ax+by+c=0$$

2. Slope of the Line
>$$slope = \frac{-a}{b}$$

3. x-intercept of the Line
>$$x-intercept = \frac{-c}{a}$$

4. y-intercept of the Line
>$$y-intercept = \frac{-c}{b}$$
