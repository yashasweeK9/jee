## Sine Rule
$$\frac{a}{\sin A} = \frac{b}{\sin B} = \frac{c}{\sin C} = 2R$$

## Cosine Rules
1. $$\cos A = \frac{b^2+c^2-a^2}{2bc}$$
2. $$\cos B = \frac{a^2+c^2-b^2}{2ac}$$
3. $$\cos B = \frac{a^2+b^2-c^2}{2ab}$$


## Triangles
1. In any $\Delta$
	1. $$\sin(A+B)=\sin C$$
	2. $$\sin(B+C)=\sin A$$
	3. $$\sin(C+A)=\sin B$$

2. Projection Formula
	1. $$a = b\cos c+c\cos b$$
	2. $$b = a\cos c+c\cos a$$
	3. $$c = a\cos b+b\cos a$$

3. Radius of In-Circle
	1. $$r = \frac{\Delta}{S}$$
		1. Where $\Delta$ is Area of the $\Delta$
		2. Where s is the Semi Perimeter $= \frac{a+b+c}{2}$
		3. $$\Delta = \sqrt{s(s-a)(s-b)(s-c)}$$ i.e. Heron's Formula
		4. $r =$ Radius of In-Circle

	> $$\Delta = \frac{1}{2}bc\sin A$$
